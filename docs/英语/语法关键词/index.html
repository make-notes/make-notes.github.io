语法关键词

时态 (Tenses):​

时态中文名示例
Present Simple现在简单时I work in an office.
Present Continuous现在进行时She is studying for her exams.
Present Perfect Simple现在完成时They have already eaten dinner.
Present Perfect Continuous现在完成进行时I have been waiting for an hour.
Past Simple过去简单时I visited London last summer.
Past Continuous过去进行时They were playing football when it started raining.
Past Perfect Simple过去完成时She had already left when I arrived.
Past Perfect Continuous过去完成进行时I had been studying for hours before the exam.
Future Simple将来简单时I will call you tomorrow.
Future Continuous将来进行时They will be arriving late.
Future Perfect Simple将来完成时By next year, I will have graduated.
Future Perfect Continuous将来完成进行时By 5 PM, they will have been working for eight hours.

词性 (Parts of Speech):​

词性中文名示例
Noun名词Dog, cat, city.
Pronoun代词I, you, he, she.
Verb动词Run, eat, sleep.
Adjective形容词Beautiful, tall, happy.
Adverb副词Quickly, always, very.
Preposition介词On, in, under.
Conjunction连词And, but, or.
Interjection感叹词Wow, oh, ouch.
Article冠词A, an, the.

句子结构 (Sentence Structure):​

分类中文名示例
Simple Sentence简单句I love learning.
Compound Sentence复合句She is smart, and she is kind.
Complex Sentence复杂句Although it was raining, we went for a walk.
Compound-Complex Sentence复合复杂句While I was studying, my friend was watching TV.

语气和语态 (Mood and Voice):​

分类中文名示例
Indicative Mood陈述语气She is reading a book.
Imperative Mood祈使语气Close the door.
Subjunctive Mood虚拟语气If I were you, I would apologize.
Active Voice主动语态The cat chased the mouse.
Passive Voice被动语态The mouse was chased by the cat.

复合词 (Compound Words):​

分类中文名示例
Noun Compound名词复合词Basketball, toothbrush.
Adjective Compound形容词复合词Cold-hearted, high-tech.
Verb Compound动词复合词Breakup, brainstorm.

连接词 (Connectives):​

分类中文名示例
Coordinating Conjunctions并列连词And, but, or.
Subordinating Conjunctions从属连词Although, because, if.
Correlative Conjunctions对应连词Either...or, neither...nor.

修辞手法 (Figures of Speech):​

分类中文名示例
Simile明喻As brave as a lion.
Metaphor暗喻Time is a thief.
Personification拟人The wind whispered through the trees.
Hyperbole夸张I've told you a million times.
Irony讽刺The fire station burned down.
Alliteration头韵Sally sells seashells by the seashore.

从句 (Clauses):​

分类中文名示例
Independent Clause独立主句She loves to read.
Dependent Clause从属主句Because it was raining, we stayed indoors.
Noun Clause名词从句What he said surprised me.
Adjective Clause形容词从句The book that he lent me is interesting.
Adverbial Clause状语从句When the bell rang, the class ended.

介词短语 (Prepositional Phrases):​

分类中文名示例
Time Prepositions时间介词短语In the morning, at night.
Place Prepositions地点介词短语On the table, under the bed.
Direction Prepositions方向介词短语Toward the mountains, into the forest.

比较和级别 (Comparison and Degrees):​

分类中文名示例
Positive Degree原级The car is fast.
Comparative Degree比较级This book is more interesting than that one.
Superlative Degree最高级It's the most beautiful place I've ever seen.

数量词 (Quantifiers):​

分类中文名示例
Some一些Can I have some water?
Many许多There are many books on the shelf.
Few少数There are few people in the park.
Several几个I'll be there in several minutes.

情态动词 (Modal Verbs):​

分类中文名示例
Can能够I can swim.
Could能够（过去）When I was younger, I could run faster.
May可能It may rain later.
Might可能（更不确定）I might go to the party.
Must必须You must finish your homework.
Shall将要Shall we go for a walk?
Should应该You should exercise regularly.
Will将要I will help you with your project.
Would将要（过去）He said he would come to the party.
Need需要You need to study for the exam.
Have to必须I have to go to work early tomorrow.

语法标点符号 (Punctuation):​

标点符号中文名示例
Period (.)句号This is a sentence.
Comma (,)逗号She went to the store, and then to the park.
Question Mark (?)问号Where are you going?
Exclamation Mark (!)感叹号Wow! That's amazing!
Colon (:)冒号There are three things I love: reading, traveling, and cooking.
Semicolon (;)分号I finished my work; now I can relax.
Apostrophe (')省略号It's a beautiful day. (It is)
Quotation Marks (" ")引号She said, "Hello."
Parentheses ()括号My dog (a golden retriever) is very friendly.
Dash (–)破折号The sky turned dark – a storm was coming.
Ellipsis (...)省略号I wonder...

主题和谓语 (Subject and Predicate):​

分类中文名示例
Subject主语The cat is sleeping.
Predicate谓语The cat is sleeping.
Compound Subject复合主语Dogs and cats are pets.
Compound Predicate复合谓语She laughed and danced.
Simple Subject简单主语The book is interesting.
Simple Predicate简单谓语She runs every morning.

形容词和副词的比较 (Comparison of Adjectives and Adverbs):​

分类中文名示例
Positive Form原级fast, tall, happy.
Comparative Form比较级faster, taller, happier.
Superlative Form最高级fastest, tallest, happiest.

名词性从句 (Noun Clauses):​

分类中文名示例
Subject Clause主语从句What he said surprised me.
Object Clause宾语从句I know where he lives.
That-Clauses以“that”引导的从句He said that he would come later.
Wh-Clauses以Wh词引导的从句I don't know who she is.

关系代词和关系副词 (Relative Pronouns and Adverbs):​

分类中文名示例
Relative Pronouns关系代词who, whom, whose, which, that.
Relative Adverbs关系副词where, when, why.

虚拟语气 (Subjunctive Mood):​

分类中文名示例
Present Subjunctive现在虚拟语气It is important that he be here on time.
Past Subjunctive过去虚拟语气If I were you, I would apologize.

非限定性从句 (Non-restrictive Clauses):​

分类中文名示例
Relative Clauses关系从句My sister, who lives in Paris, is coming to visit.
Non-restrictive Adjective Clauses非限定性形容词从句My friend, who is a doctor, helped me.

语言功能 (Functions in Language):​

分类中文名示例
Asking for Information请求信息What is your name?
Giving Advice给建议You should take a break.
Making Requests提出请求Can you pass the salt, please?
Describing Possibility描述可能性It may rain later.
Expressing Certainty表达确定性She will be here soon.
Making Predictions做出预测I think it will snow tomorrow.
Expressing Regret表达遗憾I'm sorry that I forgot your birthday.

时态和语态的混合 (Mixed Tenses and Voices):​

分类中文名示例
Mixed Tenses时态混合While I was studying, my friend called.
Mixed Voices语态混合The book is being read by many people.

描写地点 (Describing Places):​

分类中文名示例
Prepositions of Place地点介词The cat is on the roof.
Descriptive Adjectives描述性形容词The city is beautiful and bustling.

文学修辞手法 (Literary Devices):​

分类中文名示例
Alliteration头韵Peter Piper picked a peck of pickled peppers.
Onomatopoeia拟声The buzzing bee flew by.
Metaphor暗喻Time is a thief.
Simile明喻As brave as a lion.
Personification拟人The wind whispered through the trees.

数词和量词 (Numerals and Quantifiers):​

分类中文名示例
Cardinal Numbers基数One, two, three.
Ordinal Numbers序数First, second, third.
Fractions分数One-half, two-thirds.
Percentages百分比50%, 75%.
Quantifiers量词Some, many, few.

比较从句 (Comparative Clauses):​

分类中文名示例
Comparative Adjectives比较级形容词Smarter, taller, more interesting.
Comparative Adverbs比较级副词Faster, more quickly, less frequently.
Comparative Nouns比较级名词The greater, the better.

疑问词 (Question Words):​

分类中文名示例
Wh-QuestionsWh疑问词What, where, when, who, whom, whose, which, why, how.
Yes/No Questions是非疑问句Are you coming?

非谓语动词 (Non-finite Verbs):​

分类中文名示例
Infinitives不定式To run, to eat, to sleep.
Gerunds动名词Running, eating, sleeping.
Participles分词Broken, written, swimming.

形容词子句 (Adjective Clauses):​

分类中文名示例
Defining Adjective Clauses限定性形容词从句The book that you lent me is interesting.
Non-defining Adjective Clauses非限定性形容词从句My friend, who is a doctor, helped me.

连词 (Conjunctions):​

分类中文名示例
Coordinating Conjunctions并列连词And, but, or.
Subordinating Conjunctions从属连词Although, because, if.
Correlative Conjunctions对应连词Either...or, neither...nor.

情态副词 (Modal Adverbs):​

分类中文名示例
Certainly确定地Certainly, I will be there on time.
Possibly可能地It will possibly rain later.
Probably很可能She is probably at home.
Surely确定地You surely remember her name.

间接引语 (Indirect Speech):​

分类中文名示例
Reporting Verbs引述动词He said, she asked, they told.
Reporting Statements引述陈述句She said that she was coming.
Reporting Questions引述疑问句He asked if I was ready.
Reporting Commands引述祈使句She told me to sit down.

强调句 (Emphatic Structures):​

分类中文名示例
Cleft Sentences分裂句It was Mary who broke the vase.
Emphatic Adverbs强调副词She completed the task quickly.
Emphatic Pronouns强调代词I myself will take care of it.
Emphatic Auxiliaries强调助动词I do enjoy a good movie.

双重否定 (Double Negatives):​

分类中文名示例
Negative Words否定词Not, never, nobody, nothing.
Double Negative Structures双重否定结构I don't want no trouble.

特殊疑问句 (Special Interrogatives):​

分类中文名示例
Wh-Questions特殊疑问句What, where, when, who, whom, whose, which, why, how.
Yes/No Questions是非疑问句Are you coming?
Alternative Questions选择疑问句Do you want tea or coffee?

被动语态 (Passive Voice):​

分类中文名示例
Present Simple Passive现在简单被动语态The book is read by many students.
Present Continuous Passive现在进行被动语态The song is being sung by the choir.
Past Simple Passive过去简单被动语态The letter was written by John.
Past Continuous Passive过去进行被动语态The movie was being watched by everyone.
Future Simple Passive将来简单被动语态The house will be cleaned tomorrow.
Future Continuous Passive将来进行被动语态By this time tomorrow, the cake will be being baked.

比喻 (Metaphor):​

分类中文名示例
Implied Metaphor隐喻The classroom was a zoo.
Extended Metaphor扩展隐喻The world is a stage, and we are all actors.
Mixed Metaphor混合隐喻We'll burn that bridge when we come to it.

谚语和俚语 (Proverbs and Idioms):​

分类中文名示例
Proverbs谚语Actions speak louder than words.
Idioms习语Kick the bucket, break a leg.

音韵联想 (Phonetic Allusion):​

分类中文名示例
Alliteration头韵Peter Piper picked a peck of pickled peppers.
Assonance內押韵How now, brown cow?
Consonance辅音搭配韵Fleet feet sweep by sleeping geese.

反讽 (Irony):​

分类中文名示例
Verbal Irony言语反讽Saying "Oh, great!" when something goes wrong.
Situational Irony情境反讽A fire station burning down.
Dramatic Irony戏剧反讽The audience knows something the characters don't.

音律 (Prosody):​

分类中文名示例
Rhyme押韵Cat, hat, bat.
Rhythm节奏The beat of a drum.
Meter韵律Iambic pentameter in poetry.

形状和颜色 (Shapes and Colors):​

分类中文名示例
Shapes形状Circle, square, triangle.
Colors颜色Red, blue, green.

时间表达 (Expressions of Time):​

分类中文名示例
Time Expressions时间表达Yesterday, today, tomorrow.
Frequency Adverbs频率副词Always, often, rarely.
Time Prepositions时间介词In, on, at.

批判性思维词汇 (Critical Thinking Vocabulary):​

分类中文名示例
Argument论点The author's argument is convincing.
Evidence证据The data provides strong evidence.
Counterargument反驳论点However, there is a counterargument.
Claim主张The main claim of the article is clear.
Premise前提The argument is based on certain premises.

信息和媒体 (Information and Media):​

分类中文名示例
Source来源The information comes from a reliable source.
Bias偏见The article has a political bias.
Fact事实It's important to distinguish facts from opinions.
Opinion意见The author expresses a strong opinion.
Credibility可信度Assess the credibility of the information.

学术写作 (Academic Writing):​

分类中文名示例
Thesis Statement论文声明The thesis statement outlines the main argument.
Introduction导论The introduction sets the tone for the paper.
Conclusion结论In conclusion, the study has important implications.
Abstract摘要The abstract summarizes the key points of the research.
Literature Review文献综述The literature review examines existing research.
Methodology方法论The methodology explains the research approach.
Results结果The results section presents the findings.
Discussion讨论The discussion interprets the results and their implications.
