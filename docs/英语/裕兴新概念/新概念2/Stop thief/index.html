一、单词讲解 New words and expressions​

while​

n．一段时间 a period of time​

For a long while we had no new of him. 很长一段时间我们没有他的消息。

I'll be back in a little while. 我马上就回来。

It took quite a while to find a hotel. 找到旅馆花了很长一段时间。

conj.当…时候／when​

He fell asleep while (he was) doing his homework. 他在写作业的时候睡着了。

conj.与此同时 at the same time; as​

While Mary was busy in the kitchen, her husband was watching TV. 玛丽在厨房忙碌的时候她的丈夫正在看电视。

表示对比或相反的情况​

I like strong tea while he prefers weak tea 我喜欢浓茶然而他喜欢淡茶。

regret​

v.后悔，遗憾，对…惋惜 regretted​

If you go now, you'll regret it. = If you go now, you will be sorry about it. 要是你现在走的话你会后悔的。

I regretted what I said at once. 我立刻后悔我说的话了。

I regretted saying it almost at once 我几乎是立刻就后悔我说的话。

regret to do sth 遗憾地要做某事​
regret doing sth 遗憾（后悔）做过某事​

I regret to tell you that you are dismissed. 很遗憾的告诉你你被解雇了。

I regretted telling you that secret. 我后悔告诉你那个秘密了。

remember to do sth 记得去做某事​
remember doing sth 记得做过某事​

Remember to mail the letter. 记得去寄这封信。

I remembered mailing the letter. 我记得寄过这封信。

forget to do sth 忘记去做某事​
forget doing sth 忘记做过某事​

She forget to mail the letter. 她忘记去寄这封信了。

I'll never forget seeing the musical in New York. 我永远不会忘记在纽约所看到的那部音乐剧。

v. 哀悼​

We deeply regretted his death. 我们对他的去世深表哀悼。

I regret to say​

I regret to tell you = I regret to inform you that… 很遗憾地通知告诉某人（正式）

far adv.非常（程度副词，修饰比较级）​

You speak far more fluently than I do. 你比我说的流畅的多了。

这类的词还有a lot, a great deal大量, even, a little, a bit, much等。

I like this story a great deal better than the other one. 我喜欢这个故事远远超过另外一个。

You work much harder than the other girls. 你比其他女孩子干活努力的多。

Can you speak a little slower? = Can you speak a bit slower？ 你能讲的更慢一点吗？

He can make calculations even faster than a calculating machine. 他计算起来甚至比计算器还要快一些。

rush​

v. 急速地来去：冲；奔 （go or come with great speed)​

  • rush out of school 冲出学校
  • rush out of the door 冲出这扇门
  • rush away 跑走了
  • Fools rush in where angels fear to tread. （谚）初生牛犊不畏虎。

Rush these letters to the post office and make them registered. 把这封信火速的送到邮局去然后以挂号的形式邮掉。

He rushed his wife off to the hospital. 他把妻子火速地送到医院去。

run 跑 
jog 慢跑 
dash 猛跑 
race 赛跑

n. 匆促；急流；冲进​

the gold rush to California in 1848. 1848年加利福尼亚的淘金热

a rush for the concert tickets 音乐会入场券的抢购热潮

(rush n. [u] 繁忙的活动时刻）

  • during the rush hour/ in the rush hour 在交通拥挤时段

Beijing Station in rush hours 在交通拥挤时段的北京站

act​

v. do sth, perform actions 行动​

Roy acted quickly and drove the bus straight at the thieves. Roy立即开着车笔直地向前冲了过去。

It is late now. We must act at once. 现在已经晚了我们必须立即行动。

We should act rigidly on the rule. 我们应该严守规则。

v. 扮演（角色），演出，演（戏剧）​

act the part of sb 扮演…的角色​

  • = play the role of sb

action n. 行动；动作；身体举动​

Actions speak louder than words．行动胜与语言。

a graceful action 优雅的举止

straight​

adj.​

  • a straight road 一条笔直的路
  • a straight line (without a bend or curve） 一条直线
  • keep a straight face 绷着脸，忍住不笑

adv. 挺直地；直接地；径直​

He was too drunk to walk straight. 他喝的太醉了不能够走直线了。

Come straight home. 直接回家

I’ll get straight to the point. Your work isn't good enough.我就直接说了你的工作并不好。

Go straight! 往前直走.

Roy开着车笔直地向前冲了过去。

get a fright 吓我一跳​

I got the fright of my life. 我吓的要命。

faint with fright 吓昏​

frighten v.使害怕，吓唬​

Sorry, I didn't mean to frighten you. 对不起我没打算吓唬你

frighten sb to death = frighten the life out of sb 把某人吓死​

The dog frightened the squire away. 这个狗把地主给吓跑了。

squire n. (旧时）乡绅，地主；（美语）治安法官，地方法官​

frightened adj. 惊吓的，害怕的​

The frightened girl was speechless. 这个吓坏的女孩说不出话来了。

He looked frightened when he spoke. 他讲话的时候他看起来很害怕。

frightening adj. 令人惊吓的​

He spoke in a frightening manner. 他以一种令人害怕的方式说话。

frightening situation 令人害怕的形势​
frightening war 令人害怕的战争​

battered adj. 由于年久，经常使用或事故等原因而走样的​

  • a battered hat 一顶变了型帽子

Your car looks rather battered. 你的车看起来可真破啊。

batter v. 猛敲，猛砸​

batter at sth​
batter on sth​

batter n. [u]面糊，糊状物​

  • pancake batter 做薄煎饼用的面糊

shortly adv. 不久，很快（无比较级）​

  • = in a short time = not long = soon
  • shortly afterwards 不久以后
  • to put it shortly 简言之

He will be back shortly. = He will be back soon. 不久以后他很快就会回来。

The temple was rebuilt shortly after the war. 战后不久这个庙就被重建了。

I’ll be with you shortly. 我很快就来。

afterward(s)​

adv. 后来，然后，以后 at a later time​

Let's go to the theatre first and have dinner afterward(s). 我们先去看戏然后再去吃饭。

I don't have time to spare. I'll see you afterward(s). 我现在没时间我以后在去看你.

adv. 今后 from now on = henceforth​

I promise never to gamble from now on. 
I promise never to gamble afterwards. 
I promise never to gamble henceforth. 
我从今以后再也不赌博了。

二、关键句型 Key Structures​

  1. 一般过去时

  1. 现在完成时

What has happened？ 发生什么事情了？

  1. used to do 过去常常做某事

  1. 被动语态

用正确的动词填空：

  1. This is what I mean (mean).Do you understand (you understand) me? 这就是我的意思你明白了吗？
  2. Years ago, he used to smoke (smoke) but he doesn't smoke( not smoke) anymore. 很多年前他吸烟但现在他不吸了。
  3. The new Town Hall was completed (complete) last week. 新的市政大厅上星期被完成的。
  4. I haven't seen (not see) him since 1995.自从1995年我就没有见过他了。
  5. She dropped (drop) her handkerchief as she was crossing (cross) the road. 她过马路的时候把手帕掉了。

四、练习 Exercises​

1. Roy Trenton used to drive a taxi .This means he___ a taxi. 
A. has stopped driving   B. is used to driving 
C. got used to driving   D. still drives 
分析： 
A.已经停止开车了。同前一句子的意思相符； B．和C．都表示“习惯开出租车，”不符合题义； D．现在仍然开出租车，与前一句的含义相反。    
B.答案：A. 

2. He noticed two thieves_____ out of a shop. 
A. to come   B. are coming  C. in coming  D. come 
分析： 
notice表示“注意到”的意思，后面跟不带to的动词不定式或现在分词作宾语补足语，即notice sb do/ doing sth. 
A.、B.、和C都不符合语法结构； 
D. 这是一个不带to的动词不定式，符合结构。 
答案：D. 

3. The car was easy to recognize, so it wasn't___ difficult for the police to catch the thieves. 
A. much   B. very  C. many D. too 
分析: D．太，符合题义；too常用于这种结构：It is too + adj.+for sb to do sth; C．只能修饰可数名词复数，不能用来修饰形容词。 
答案：D. 

4. They traveled to Spain by the most____ route. 
A. easy   B. direct  C. straight D. unique 
分析： 
A．容易的，不费力的；舒服的，安逸的；B．径直的，直接的： 
C．诚实的，正直的，直的； D．唯一的，独特的。 答案：B.
